Signature Dishes

TANDOORI MISAL

Let’s face it. We love Tandoori. In everything. Which is why we haven’t let our beloved misal stay behind. A beautiful mix of traditional tandoori spices mixed with gravy, farsan, onions and coriander topped with a whiff of smoke. Aapli Special aahe! Try kara.

KOLHAPURI MISAL

For all you ‘thickat’ lovers out there. This familiar blend of beans tempered with super-hot Kolhapuri masala potatoes and a special type of farsan will blow you away. Don’t curse us next morning.

TANDOORI CHAI

A drink fit for the chai lovers and Tandoori lovers alike. Lovely milk tea infused with Tandoori smoke and flavour to make your senses wonder what just happened.

NASIK MISAL

They do it thoda hatkar. Their black masala makes this dish intriguing in taste. But when the deliciousness is all that we aim for, colour ka kya karna hai?
